Delete Your Frydgi Account

This page explains how to delete your Frydgi account and associated data.

In-App Deletion

  1. Open the Frydgi app
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm

What Gets Deleted

Your account and all related data are permanently deleted, including:

  • Pantry items
  • Recipes and meal plans
  • Shopping lists
  • Preferences and usage data tied to your account

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Need Help?

If you can’t access the app, email us at bpalmer@myseniorfirst.com with the subject line Delete My Account.
